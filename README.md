# familytreeapp-legal

Public legal & support pages for the **Kintree** app (`familytreeapp`), served via GitHub Pages.

- Privacy Policy — `/privacy`
- Terms of Service — `/terms`
- Support — `/support`

The privacy policy and terms are **placeholders** while the full text (kept in the
private app repo under `docs/legal/`) is finalized and reviewed by counsel before launch.
